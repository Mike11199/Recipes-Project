Pina Colada

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup ice
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapple, frozen
  • 2 ounces pineapple juice
  • 2 ounces Coco Lopez coconut cream
  • 1 1/2 ounces white run
  • 1 ounce dark rum
  • pineapple slices

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glass and serve garnished with pineapple slice and cherry.
